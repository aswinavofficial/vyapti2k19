WET 'N' WILD

Presented by Department of Mechanical Engineering

 


Problem Statement

The participants have to build a land bot and a water bot. The water bot has to pick up blocks kept in water and safely carry it to land, at the same time the land bot has to navigate through the arena to the prescribed spot to pick up these blocks. The land bot has to carry the blocks one at a time and place it in the gaps to complete the bridge. On completion of the bridge, the land bot has to navigate to the finish point to complete the event

Arena description

The arena consists of both land and water.
The land area is divided into two sub areas separated by a gap where the participants have to build a bridge. The base land area is at a height of 2cm from the water level. The land bot has to climb a slope of 45 degrees to reach the platform to pick up the blocks placed by the water bot in the base land area. The platform is at a height of 14cm from the water level. On completing the bridge, the land bot has to climb a slope of 60 degrees to reach the finish point.
The water bot has to pick up the blocks from a platform at a height of 1cm from the water level and place it in the base land area.
The block dimensions are 6cm x 6cm x 6cm


Rules

  1. The robots can be wired or wireless
  2. Maximum team members is 5
  3. The max dimensions should be restricted to:
    Land bot - 30cm x 30cm x 30cm (excluding the attachment for picking blocks)
    Water bot – 25cm x 25cm x 25cm (excluding the attachment for picking blocks)
  4. Any type of design damaging the arena would be disqualified
  5. The total time for the event is 15 minutes

Points criteria

Climbing 45 degrees slope - 10 points
Picking a block kept in water 5 points
Picking a block kept on land – 8 points Crossing the bridge - 15 points
Climbing 60 degrees slope – 20 points Balance Time * 30 points
Design of robot – 10 points(max) PRIZE WORTH 30000
