REEL IT IN

Presented by Department of Computer Science and Engineering

 

“Let me just pause a minute and drink in this moment. And if you film it, I’ll be able to get free refills for life.” -Jarod Kintz
 
Is movie making your passion? Are you the one who goes crazy about movies? Then this is the right opportunity for you.
 
How It Works ?
● First a registration form is send out (google form).
● Only the registered people can participate.
● We then send an email to the registered candidates the guidelines to be followed.
● Later send an email to the participants to send their short film video or Youtube link as the reply.
● A panel of judges will view the films and mark accordingly.
● The film with highest mark wins.
● We can show the top 3 or 4 movies during the techfest in a room and do a voting. The movie with highest votes can also be given an award of audience choice. Guidelines to participants:
● Participants should send the video’s youtube link to the google form.
● Director or producer of the film should only participate in the short film.
● Winners will be intimated through email.(Winners should bring their photo ID proof).
● Everyone participating will get a certificate of participation.
● The competition is open to all.
● The films should be released after 1st Jan 2018.
● The judgement will be based on
 o Direction
 o Cinematography
 o Acting
 o Script
 o BGM
● The best film will be awarded Rs 5000/- .
● Registration fee is Rs 200/-.
