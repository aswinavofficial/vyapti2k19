TECH QUIZ

Presented by Department of Computer Science and Engineering

 
It is a technical quiz to test your knowledge about computers.
 
It will check if you know the logos, taglines, famous personalities who have contributed to technology , many trending technologies, etc.
 
Quiz will be conducted in 5 levels:
(Every question in each levels will have time limit - around 60 seconds)
 
Level 1
Identify the Logo (s/w, programming language, development tool... Etc ), tag lines.
 
Level 2
Identify the person (founder, CEO, scientist.. Etc)
 
Level 3
Full-Form of abbreviations
 
Level 4
Shortcut keyboard keys
 
Level 5
Name the technology to be used in the given scenario (we'll provide a scenario. You have to choose an apt technology for the same )
 
Prize Money : Rs 5K
No of participants : 2 Members per team.
Registration fee : Rs. 40 per team
Date : 24th March 2019
Venue : Steve Jobs Hall ( 304 )
Time : 10:00am
 
For Your Information:
  • No level by level elimination, Winner will decided upon the Best scores secured after all 5 levels.
  • Prelims will be conducted only if no. of registered teams exceeds 10 .
  • Prelims will be conducted one week prior the actual event (dates announced later and top ten teams will proceed for the actual quiz).
  • Spot registration possible if no. Of registered team less than 10.
