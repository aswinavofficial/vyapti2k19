CRACQ'EM

Presented by Department of Basic Science and Humanities

 

Cracq'em is a technology driven event.Here puzzles of various kinds from questions to solving equations are inscribed in QR codes and pasted at various locations at the college. Participants in team of two should hunt the QR codes, scan them using their phones and answer it in the sheet of paper provided.
Time : about an hour.
Registration fee : ₹20 per team
Venue : 10:00am @ M324
Prize :
 1st Prize ₹ 2500
 2nd Prize ₹ 1500
 3rd Prize ₹ 1000
