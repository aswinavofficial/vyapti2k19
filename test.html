About

ABOUT VYAPTI

Muthoot Institute of Technology & Science proudly presents first ever Inter Collegiate Technical Fest VYAPTI 2k19. Celebrating Technology and innovation, VYAPTI is the complete technical extravaganza with promising prodigious quality. VYAPTI provides the best tribune for multitude of students to showcase their Technical valour.

Venue

Now you have the location

Muthoot Institute of Technology And Science, Varikoli

Muthoot Institute of Technology and Science (MITS) is a self financing Engineering College is situated in the Industrial suburb of KOCHI close to the Smart City and Info Park, approximately 15 kms. from Vytilla Junction towards Muvattupuzha on the Cochin Madurai National Highway.
