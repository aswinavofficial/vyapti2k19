7TH VICTIM

Presented by Department of Civil Engineering

 


There has been six serial killings and there will be one more murder, the participants must use their logical and technological skills to solve different clues and find the seventh person before he becomes the seventh victim.

Event mainly consist of mainly 4 rounds excluding one preliminary round if the no of participants is more than expected.
Round 1 Here participants are given with the details of the previous six killings. From that reports the contestants are expected to crack a clue which will lead to a computer which has the data for subsequent rounds. (Only 7 teams are selected for next rounds)

Round 2 Contestants are required to crack the password for entering their own respective computers in the second round, from that computer they will get the data which contains the location of the dark room (which is killer’s planning room).

Round 3 There will be 7 teams in this round and they are required to find the place of the expected seventh killing and save them,not only that they should also be able to find the story behind it and they can leave the dark room through a single door which is locked.The key for that room is also hidden in this room and there will be clues for the location of the keys in the room.

Round 4 This is the final round which is the place of the expected 7th killing and before entering to this room the contestants are required to explain the story they have understood from the previous rounds and who was the 7th victim?



The first 3 teams who will find this location and the story will be the winners.



Prizes worth 15k
