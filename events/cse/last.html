LAST ONE STANDING

Presented by Department of Computer Science and Engineering

 

After centuries, researchers finally developed a general AI !!!

When they found out what it was capable of doing, people slowly began to give the AI more responsibilities and tasks.
Slowly and peacefully it accepted its tasks and everything went smoothly as people could assign painstaking tasks like controlling world peace, traffic management, health issues... etc and almost every other thing we can imagine to the AI. Day by day AI got more knowledge and power. And when it got access to world history and human behaviour for the betterment of our world itself, the unthinkable happened. The AI got into a conclusion that humans are the main threat to Earth. And the only way it can save the World is by taking over the world and controlling every nation by itself, which was not allowed by the people. So, it figured out that wiping out human race is the only solution to the problem, which the AI planned to do by itself without informing anyone else.
A smart one among the researchers who was not really happy to give the AI control, figured out AI’s plan and obviously he/she tried to stop it. But as the AI was keeping an eye on her/him AI captured and locked him/her with the help of bots and they wiped his/her memory partially. But as she/he was expecting a move like this from the AI he/she was prepared to face it by putting clues around the AI’s core building’s surroundings. Now it’s your turn to play as the ONE!!!

The ONE who fights back! Stands up for justice and peace!!!

In this event in which you’ll be racing against time before the AI does the unthinkable, it’s your duty to escape from AI’s prison and find way to AI’s core server room and shut it down for the better.


Team: 4 members

Prize Money: 5K

INSTRUCTIONS FOR CONTESTANTS
 This will be a time bound event, contestants will have 2 hours to solve the clues/riddles of each level to reach the final level. Levels will be logical and slightly CS oriented (Towards the end).
 The event will be guided with the help of an app, which will be installed on the contestants’ smartphone by volunteers.
 Contestants can form a team of 4.
 It will have 5 main levels and many sub levels, which the contestants might have to participate with respect to their performance in the main levels.
 At least one of the team members should have a working android smartphone.
 Winners will be selected by analysing the time taken for each task and the percentage of completion of the event.
 Some clues, map and hints will be provided within the app.
 Decision of event co-ordinators will be final.

Registration fee: Rs.100/Team
