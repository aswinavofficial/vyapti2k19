TECH SENATE

Presented by Department of Electrical & Electronics Engineering

 
Venue : Visvesvaraya Hall
Prize Worth : 15k
Fee : 300 rupees per group
Team Size : 4-6 students per Team.

EVENT DESCRIPTION

Make a "bill" for the parliament!!!
 
Coordinate and arrange your plan.
 
If you have the idea……if you have the courage to present the bill before the parliament……come out with it and knock the door!!!
 
The paper presented in parliament as bill is based on the theme innovations in science and technology. Group size is confined to 4-6 members. Speaker will handle the entire parliament. The invited groups will be given a chance to present their bill (idea or project) in front of the parliament. The other groups can scrutinize the bill after the presentation followed by a debating session to oppose and support the bill.
 
Voting session will be arranged to vote for the bill where a group can vote for the best bill except theirs.
 
Exciting prize will be awarded to the best bill selected by the jury and bill with maximum votes. Also there will be an attractive prize for the best performer of the senate.
 
Why to miss this senate?
 
So….Kit your ideas out!!! Present your bill!!! Grab the prize!!!
