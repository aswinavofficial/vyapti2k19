KAKURO

Presented by Department of Basic Science and Humanities

 

Kakuro is a kind of logic puzzle.It is a mathematical transliteration of the crossword. It is one round event. Two or three puzzles shall be printed on a sheet of paper and participants in teams of two can answer in the given sheet itself.
Time : about an hour.
Registration fee : ₹20 per team
Venue : 10:00am @ M315
Prize :
 1st Prize ₹ 1000
 2nd Prize ₹ 600
 3rd Prize ₹ 400
