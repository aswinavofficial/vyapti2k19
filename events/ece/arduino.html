ARDUINO ENZINIUS

Presented by Department of Electronics and Communications Engineering

 

VYAPTI 2K19 is giving you an opportunity to outshine your ability. Here we’re up with the event ARduino ENZINIUS which looks forward for the young talented brains.Be here to conquer the battle!!

Prize Money worth : 6K
Fee : 200 per Team
Team Size : 4 Members

EVENT DESCRIPTION

The event consists of 3 rounds. Each round is an elimination round.
Round 1 :

In the first round the team will be split into 2. One pair will stand at the venue and the other will have to hunt for their questions. The pairs can be interchanged if needed after every clue. The teams to collect the most number of questions and answer them correctly will be selected for the next round. Top 10-15 teams will be shortlisted.

Round 2 :

The shortlisted teams will be entered into a dark room from where they will have to find their problem Statement, pen and paper. The teams will have to code their problem statement within the time the candle burns off. Top 5-7 teams will be selected for the next round.

Round 3 :

The participants will be provided with components, of which they have to build there own obstacle avoider line following robot. The arena will be provided .The obstacle avoider line follower robot have to make its way from the start point to the exit. Based on the time and performance of the robot, winners will be announced.

RULES

  1. Each team maximum of 4 students.
  2. College ID cards are mandatory.
  3. Team members should be from the same college.
  4. The event consists of 3 rounds.
  5. Each round is an elimination round.
  6. Participants will be judged based on the timing and performance of the robot.
  7. Electronic gadgets are not allowed.
  8. Judges decision will be final
