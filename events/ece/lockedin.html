LOCKED INN

Presented by Department of Electronics and Communications Engineering

 

Now it’s time to push you to the limit. Have you got the art of escaping? Can you conquer your fear of darkness? Then get yourself locked in and we will give you techs to escape. Circuits will be your keys, logic will be your escape rule and observation will assist your moves.

Prize Money worth : 5K
Fee : 200 per Team
Team Size :3 or 4 Members

EVENT DESCRIPTION

LOCKED IN is an event where you can experience the fear of getting locked. Here you will be trapped inside a dark room where you have to use your skills to escape. You need to have keen observation, use all your senses to find your way out. Watch out for the dangers seeking within, it may lead you to wrong directions because every end is a new beginning of another. So, prepare yourself for locked in.

RULES

  1. Each team must contain team of 3 and maximum of 4.
  2. No electronic gadgets allowed.
  3. Do not damage or alter any of the props and equipment.
  4. Winners will be selected prioritising the number of level they complete and on time basis.
